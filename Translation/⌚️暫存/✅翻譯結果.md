The cash flow statement shows you the actual cash a company is pocketing.

If you're into value investing, the cash flow statement is a must-read because it reveals how much money a business is actually bringing home. In this post, we’ll break down the three main types of activities (operating, investing, and financing) and show you how to calculate free cash flow (FCF). You'll learn how to tell if those earnings on paper are actually turning into real cash, giving you a clear picture of a company's true profitability.
